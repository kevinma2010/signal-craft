# Code of Conduct

SignalCraft's community conduct standards are defined in the
[Community Conduct](PROJECT_POLICY.md#community-conduct) section of the
project policy.

In short: be respectful and specific, critique ideas rather than people,
provide evidence for strong claims, and disclose conflicts of interest
when relevant. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate
disruption, doxxing, and repeated bad-faith participation are unacceptable.

To report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers privately through
GitHub rather than opening a public issue.
