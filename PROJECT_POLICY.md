# Project Policy

This document summarizes governance, community conduct, privacy principles, acknowledgements, and trademark usage for SignalCraft.

## Governance

SignalCraft currently follows a lead-maintainer model.

SignalCraft was created by **Kevin Ma**, who serves as founder and lead maintainer. **PATHLINK DIGITAL LLC** is the copyright owner and long-term operating entity behind the project.

The lead maintainer is responsible for:

- Product direction
- Architecture decisions
- Release approval
- Source policy
- Security response
- Maintainer appointments

Meaningful changes to architecture, governance, privacy, licensing, or source policy should be discussed through GitHub Issues or Discussions when practical.

Consistent contributors may be invited to become maintainers.

## Community Conduct

SignalCraft aims to maintain a professional, constructive, and technically rigorous community.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and specific
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people
- Provide evidence for strong claims
- Welcome good-faith questions
- Respect privacy and confidential information
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, doxxing, and repeated bad-faith participation are unacceptable.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

## Privacy Principles

SignalCraft should minimize the collection and retention of personal data.

The following should remain local whenever practical:

- User interests
- Custom topics
- Read history
- Feedback history
- Delivery configuration
- Delivery credentials
- Custom prompts

Credentials should never be logged and should use the narrowest practical scope.

Any telemetry must be disclosed clearly and should be optional.

A future hosted SignalCraft service will publish a separate privacy policy.

## Acknowledgements

SignalCraft was partly inspired by the lightweight agent-based digest format of the open-source project **Follow Builders, Not Influencers**.

SignalCraft is independently designed and implemented, with a broader focus on:

- Topic-driven discovery
- Primary-source intelligence
- Evidence-backed summaries
- Cross-source ranking
- Dynamic source discovery
- Story clustering
- Personalized relevance

No code, prompts, onboarding text, examples, or documentation from the referenced project should be copied into SignalCraft unless the reuse is explicitly documented and all applicable license obligations are followed.
